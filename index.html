Welcome!

Ji Lab, headed by Prof. Xiaoyun Ji, is located in School of Life Science at Nanjing University. Our laboratory studies infectious disease-related viruses and the host immune response. We use a variety of techniques, including X-ray crystallography, electron microscopy, molecular biology, cell biology, and computational biology.
